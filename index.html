Rules

  • A team comprise of two participants.
  • The participants will be graded based solely on the marks scored as well as the exact amount of time taken for completing a particular round.
  • The Round 2(Day 2) will involve 3 subsequent levels. A team won’t be able to move to any further level(s) without completing the former level(s).
  • No internet access will be provided.
  • Use of pen-drives, e-books, any other soft material or internet is restricted. Any team found engaged in such activities will be immediately disqualified.
  • In case of a tie, the decision of the coordinators will be final and bound to all.

Schedule

  • Pre-Round: 16th March 2016, 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm.
  • Final Round: 18th March 2016.

Event Description

    The world is witnessing an outrageous situation of cold war; countries are secretly trying to wage cyber wars against each other. In order to stop this, the UN’s artificial intelligence unit has installed an AI on the Internet. Upon activation, this AI immediately perceived the human race as a “security threat”, and formulated a plan to systematically take control over humanity and wipe out the entire human race. This AI is hacking into the systems going by the name of ‘ULTRON’ and hence initiating unprecedented cyber-attacks by deploying viruses over the internet. Governments’ confidential documents, secret mission information, N-level details, stock exchanges, bank databases….nothing is left unaffected. The UN has devised a Resistance Force to combat with the Ultron…. your team is one of them. Get ready to defeat the AI!!....trace it, demobilise it, and finally aim to breach its digital fortress.
